考研复试自我介绍英文模板（李旭）

发表于 2020-03-07 更新于 2020-03-08 分类于 考研 ， 英语 阅读次数： Valine：
本文字数： 7.5k 阅读时长 ≈ 7 分钟

Sample 1

  Good morning, Sir. I’m very glad to be here for this interview. My name is XXX. I come from XXX, the capital city of XXX. I graduated from XXX, and my major is XXX. I like literature, especially traditional Chinese Literature. I wish to learn more here.

  I am an active girl, quick-thinking and very fond of music. I usually go to the concert with my friends in my spare time. Besides, I am very interested in learning English. I have passed CET-6, and I got 6.5 in the IELTS test. I desire to learn more, so if I’m given an opportunity to study in this university, I will try my best to make great achievement. Thank you.

Sample 2

  Good morning, professor. It’s my honor to be here. My name is XXX. I was born in Xi’an, which is famous for the Terracotta Warriors, and also it’s the beginning of the Silk Road.

  I graduated from Xi’an University of Technology, majoring in Material Physics. After graduation, I worked in a car company as a common employee.But I found my job unsatisfying. I loved painting when I was little. But my family could not afford to pay for the expensive pigment, tools and tuition.Working hard for a few years, I have made some savings. So I decided to turn to painting both in study and for future. I have also been taking classes in the studio until now. I hope that I could make an achievement in the painting field. Thank you so much.

Sample 3

  Good afternoon, everyone. First let me introduce myself. I’m XXX, 22 years old. My hometown is in Changchun, which located in the northeast of China, and it’s very cold in winter. I graduated from Tsinghua University, majoring in Vehicle Engineering. I like all kinds of cars, and I am also crazy about making cars. I even went to the auto repair factory to do some part-time jobs with my classmates. But when I finished my study, I realized that what I have learned is not enough. I would like to further my study, so I choose to continue my interest with a master’s degree. I’m an optimistic, energetic and hard-working man. If I’m given an opportunity to continue my study here, I will spare no effort to be a qualified graduate student. Thank you for your attention.

Sample 4

  Hello, everyone. I’m XXX from Dalian Ocean University, majoring in Aquaculture. Dalian is a coastal city, so the main landscapes are the beaches and bays. There are also a few nice hills nearby. I have studied here for four years. I love the people and beautiful sight here. So I decide to go on with my study here. In my spare time, I always go to the aquarium, where rare submarines are displayed. I like to watch them and want to know more about their habits. It is my interest and hard work that enabled me to publish several academic papers. I believe that with my determination to deepen my study, I can make great achievement in my academic career. That’s all. Thank you so much.

Sample 5

  Good morning. It is my pleasure to have the opportunity for this interview. First, let me introduce myself briefly. My name is XXX. I graduated from Peking University, which is quite famous for its Economics.

  My hometown is a small village in Qingdao. The villagers are mostly farmers, who are warm-hearted and friendly, but a bit conservative. But I grew up in a quite open family. Although my parents are common workers, they are open-minded. They tried their best to give me a good education, and they respected my opinions. So I could come here to go on with my study while many of my mates in my village get married and try their best to support their families. I thank my parents for their love and care. I will do my best to study here. That is the best reward for them. Thanks.

Sample 6

  Good morning, professors. I have been expecting this interview since I got the admission. I’m XXX from Tibet. I’m 22 years old. I graduated from Tibet University. Since I entered my university, I have been studying hard. I was granted scholarship every semester. If I could be lucky to have this opportunity to study here, I would concentrate on my study, and learn as much as possible. After three years of study, I will go back to my hometown. Since Tibet is a poor place without adequate basic facilities and infrastructures both in education and medical care, a lot of qualified graduates are needed. I feel it’s part of my responsibility to make my hometown a better place. Thank you.

Sample 7

  Good morning, dear professor. Thank you for giving me the opportunity to have this interview. I’m XXX, coming from a beautiful ancient city, Huizhou, Anhui Province. I graduated from Anhui Normal University, majoring in Mathematics. During my four years of study, I was granted the first-class scholarship every semester. I have mastered both basic and professional skills in Mathematics. And I have passed CET-4 and CET-6 in my sophomore year. I also did part-time jobs in my spare time.

  The knowledge I learned from university helped me a lot in my work. If I am lucky enough to be a student of this great university, I will try my best to develop my abilities in related aspects and try to be a qualified graduate. That’s all about me. Thank you.

Sample 8

  Hello, everyone. It is really a great honor to have this opportunity for an interview. I hope I can make a good impression today. My name is XXX. I graduated from XXX University, which located in the famous city Guangzhou. I’m 22. There are five people in my family: my parents, my grandmother, my little sister and me. As for my hobbies and interests, I like swimming, skiing, Chinese chess and dancing. When I was a freshman in the university, I joined a dancing union and finally became the minister through my hard work. During the two years dancing with my schoolmates, I developed the ability of organizing and learned how to take a leadership role. Thank you for your time and attention.

Sample 9

  Good morning. It’s my great honor to introduce myself. I’m XXX. I graduated from Tongji university, majoring in Power Engineering. I come from Shanghai, which is a busy city with rapid development. Youngsters and workers from other cities come to my city, seeking for good opportunities and wish to make a better living. Now Shanghai is really a crowded place to live, with extremely high price of apartments. Food, clothes and other commodities are also expensive. My parents worked so hard to afford my tuition. I cherish my learning opportunity so much. So I will do my best to perform well today, and make efforts to have great achievement in the near future. Thank you for giving me the chance.

Sample 10

  Good morning. It’s my honor to be here. First, I would like to introduce myself briefly. My name is XXX. I come from Chongqing. It is a hot and humid city. All the people there like spicy food. I am also keen on spicy food, which is necessary in my everyday diet. Chongqing is also a beautiful place for tourists. There are many places of interest, such as the Yangtze River Cableway. And there are also a variety of special snacks which you cannot find in other places.

  As for my major, I graduated from Sichuan Conservatory of Music, majoring in Piano Performing. Li Yundi is my schoolmate. He is my idol, and I dream of becoming a great pianist in the near future just like him. That’s all. Thank you.
